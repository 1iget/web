The OpenSSL Software Foundation represents the OpenSSL project in most legal capacities including, contributor license agreements, managing donations, and so on. It is a Delaware (US) non-profit corporation with its own bylaws.

OpenSSL Software Services represents the OpenSSL project as needed for selected commercial contexts, such as providing formal support contracts. It is a Delaware (US) corporation with its own bylaws.
